About XPRTIS INSPIRE Program

XPRTIS INSPIRE Program offers motivated high school students interested in software engineering, data science, robotics, design, and business the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to real-world problem-solving through carefully selected projects from our active portfolio. Each year, we carefully interview and select students based on their skills, mindset, and motivation. Those chosen will have the opportunity to collaborate with experienced professionals, research teams, and mission-driven organizations on impactful, real-world initiatives.

Every year, we interview and select qualified students based on their skillsets, character, and motivation. Selected students are given the opportunity to collaborate with established enterprises, research-focused teams, and purpose-driven organizations while gaining hands-on experience and building a strong professional foundation.
