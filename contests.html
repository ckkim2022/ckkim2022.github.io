Discover the Contest with XPRTIS

The XPRTIS Program offers a wide variety of contests and initiatives tailored for high school students, ranging from logic competitions to cutting-edge technovation challenges. Each year, more than 4,000 students participate, showcasing their creativity, problem-solving abilities, and technical skills. Our contests are designed to inspire innovation, encourage collaboration, and provide students with valuable real-world experience. Whether you're passionate about coding, critical thinking, or entrepreneurship, the XPRTIS Program provides an exciting platform to challenge yourself, grow, and connect with like-minded peers.

Technovation South Korea

Technovation South Korea is an exciting initiative that empowers young women to become changemakers through technology. If you're passionate about solving real-world problems, this program invites you to sign up and make an impact. Participants will work in teams to develop mobile apps tackling critical social issues such as climate change, mental health, and education, with expert mentorship. Since its inception, Technovation has empowered over 23,000 girls across 115 countries to become tech entrepreneurs and leaders.

United States Science & Engineering Olympiad (USSEO)

USSEO is an innovation hub focused on human-centered design, encouraging participants to develop impactful solutions in areas such as accessibility, sustainability, and emergency services. This year marks the first year the challenge is partnering with universities, offering students the opportunity to engage in global collaboration and creative problem-solving. Competitors can choose from a variety of themed events, work with familiar tools like VEX and FIRST, and participate in both online and in-person rounds.
