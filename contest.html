Discover the Contest with XPRTIS

The XPRTIS Program offers a wide variety of contests and initiatives tailored for high school students, ranging from logic competitions to cutting-edge technovation challenges. Each year, more than 1,000 students participate, showcasing their creativity, problem-solving abilities, and technical skills. Our contests are designed to inspire innovation, encourage collaboration, and provide students with valuable real-world experience. Whether you're passionate about coding, critical thinking, or entrepreneurship, the XPRTIS Program provides an exciting platform to challenge yourself, grow, and connect with like-minded peers.

Technovation Korea

Technovation Korea is an exciting initiative that empowers young women to become changemakers through technology. If you're passionate about solving real-world problems, this program invites you to sign up and make an impact. Participants will work in teams to develop mobile apps tackling critical social issues such as climate change, mental health, and education, with expert mentorship.

International Logic Olympiad

The International Logic Olympiad is a premier logic competition held annually by LUMINAS in partnership with Stanford University to promote and globalize logic education. We will collaborate with LUMINAS for future cooperation.
