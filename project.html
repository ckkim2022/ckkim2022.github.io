About XPRTIS INSPIRE Program

XPRTIS INSPIRE Program was launched to give high school students with interests in software engineering, data science, robotics, design, and business unique opportunities to join a few selected projects from our ongoing portfolio and make practical contributions in solving real-world problems.

Every year, we interview and select qualified students based on their skillsets, personality, and motivation. Selected students will be given the opportunity to collaborate not only with renowned enterprises and institutions such as the subsidiary of Samsung and research laboratories of UC Berkeley and Carnegie Mellon, but also with global organizations such as the Red Cross.
