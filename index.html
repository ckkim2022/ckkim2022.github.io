XPRTIS INSPIRE PROGRAM

The XPRTIS INSPIRE Program invites passionate high school students to step into real-world innovation across software engineering, data science, robotics, design, and business. Each year, we carefully select students who show strong potential—not just in skills, but in mindset and motivation. Those accepted join collaborative projects with leading organizations such as Samsung’s Chiels, research teams at UC Berkeley and Carnegie Mellon, and humanitarian groups like the Red Cross. By contributing directly to high-impact work, students gain practical experience, build lasting skills, and shape a portfolio that reflects their ability to lead and create meaningful change.
