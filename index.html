XPRTIS INSPIRE PROGRAM

The XPRTIS INSPIRE Program is designed for high school students with a drive for software engineering, data science, robotics, design, and business, giving them hands-on experience in real-world problem-solving. Each year, we handpick students based on their skills, personality, and motivation, connecting them with top-tier projects from our portfolio. Those selected get the chance to collaborate with industry leaders like Samsung’s Chiels and research powerhouses at UC Berkeley and Carnegie Mellon, as well as global organizations like the Red Cross. Through professional, high-impact projects, students gain practical skills, explore fresh ideas, and make meaningful contributions that set the stage for a future in development and beyond.
